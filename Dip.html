Overview

We know how annoying filling out details section could be, it takes time and effort to fill out the complete details section. Hence I came with an idea of improving the overall experience of filling out details section for an Health & Wellness app. The primary focus of the details section in this app is to collect personal data such as: Age, Height, Weight etc. so that it can be used to personalise the further experience in the app such as analyzing calorie intake, diet, BMI etc. My primary focus was on to minimize user's effort & enhancing his experience while filling out the form. Hence the methodology was to inculcate a stream line questionnaire along with selection of attributes.
